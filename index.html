ℹ️ About
BUFT Unified Bus Tracker V3.0

A comprehensive student-led idea designed to modernize University transportation.

Key Features:
  • Real-Time Map Tracking: Instantly see the exact, live location of all university buses on an interactive map.
  • Route Identification: Easily find which bus is heading to your destination (e.g., Mirpur, Tongi, Azampur, etc.) by tapping the bus icons.
  • Live Status & Speed: Check if your bus is currently moving, stopped, or inactive, along with its current speed in km/h.
  • Driver Information: View the assigned driver's name and contact information (if available) directly from the map.
  • Mobile-Optimized: Designed to work smoothly on your smartphone so you can track your ride while on the go.
🚀 What's New in V3.0 ▼
  • Crowdsourced Capacity: Real-time "Mostly Full" and "Full" passenger reports to help you plan your ride.
  • Live Compass: "Locate Me" features a rotating blue beam showing your exact direction.
  • Embedded Labels: Bus numbers sit perfectly centered on map icons for instant reading.
  • True Dark Mode: A sleek menu switch automatically darkens the app and map tiles.
  • Sidebar Search: Instantly find specific buses or routes without scrolling.
  • Dynamic Colors: Icons and dots accurately reflect running, stopped, or inactive states.
  • Readable Times: Inactive durations are formatted cleanly (e.g., "Inactive for 22h 06m").
