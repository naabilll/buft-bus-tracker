ℹ️ About
BUFT Unified Bus Tracker V4.0

A comprehensive student-led idea designed to modernize University transportation.

Key Features:
  • Live Map: See exactly where all buses are right now.
  • Smart Colors: Bus icons are colored (Green=Moving, Yellow=Idle, Red=Offline) so you know their status.
  • Find Your Bus: Click any bus to see its driver, phone number, and route.
  • Get Directions: Tap "Catch Bus" to walk to your bus, or "Route to BUFT" to navigate to campus.
  • Crowd Updates: See if a bus is full, and report it to help others!
🚀 What's New in V4.0 ▼
  • Live Traffic: Check for road jams directly on the map with the new traffic toggle.
  • In-App Navigation: "Catch Bus" and "Route to BUFT" now show the exact driving/walking path directly inside a pop-up window!
  • Satellite View: New toggle to see real-world satellite imagery of the roads.
  • Cleaner Layout: Bus popups are now much more compact, modern, and easy to read.
